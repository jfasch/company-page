# Copy Original Image to File

Insert the SD card into you card reader. It should be recognized by
the kernel and thus appear in `/proc/partitions`

~~~
$ cat /proc/partitions 
major minor  #blocks  name

   8        0  234431064 sda
   8        1     262144 sda1
   8        2    8388608 sda2
   8        3  225779288 sda3
   8       32   61120512 sdb
   8       33     270336 sdb1
~~~

You should probably check `/proc/partitions` before and after you
insert the card to see which device is the card. Here,

* `sda` is my entire internal disk.
* `sda{1,2,3}` are the three partitions
* jjj noobs installieren, dann kamma schaun wie die partitions sind

cp /dev/sdb ~/small.img

(jjj no need to use dd)
